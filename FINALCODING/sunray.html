Sunray For Sale

The spectacular 85m motor yacht SUNRAYS was delivered in 2010 by the acclaimed Dutch shipyard Oceanco. Built to a Bjorn Johansson design, she is the 5th Hull of their impressive Y700 series.



With interior styling by the renowned designer Terence Disdale, every inch of SUNRAYS exudes luxury. Split over six decks, her multi-functional interior layout has been carefully considered for entertaining. Of her many highlights, the spacious main salon offering a lounge and formal dining area, while the bridge deck is home to a lavish spa and a lounge that converts into a home cinema.
